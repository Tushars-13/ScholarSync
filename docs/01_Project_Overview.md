# ScholarSync

## What is ScholarSync?

ScholarSync is an AI-powered College ERP platform designed to provide a single digital ecosystem for students, teachers, TNP departments, and administrators.

The platform aims to modernize traditional college ERP systems by combining academics, communication, placements, analytics, and AI-powered automation into one application.

---

## Why are we building it?

Traditional ERP systems are outdated, difficult to use, and split important services across multiple portals.

ScholarSync aims to solve these problems by creating one modern, responsive, and intelligent platform.
