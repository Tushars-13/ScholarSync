# Dashboard UI Design Decisions (26 June 2026)

## Design Philosophy

ScholarSync follows a modern SaaS-inspired dashboard design focused on simplicity, usability, and consistency.

The goal is to create a professional student portal that feels like a real-world product rather than a college project.

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Color

- #2563EB

Used for:
- Navbar
- Active Navigation
- Important Buttons
- Primary Actions

---

### Background

- #F8FAFC

Provides a soft background that reduces eye strain compared to pure white.

---

### Card Background

- #FFFFFF

Used for dashboard cards and information panels.

---

### Text Colors

Heading:
- #1F2937

Secondary Text:
- #64748B

---

## Typography

Primary Font:
- Inter

Reason:
- Modern
- Highly readable
- Widely used in professional SaaS products

---

## Icons

Library:
- React Icons

Reason:
- Consistent SVG icons
- Lightweight
- Professional appearance
- Better than emoji icons
